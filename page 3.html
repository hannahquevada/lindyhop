Our Local Lindy Hop Community

Singapore's Lindy Hop community is small but strong. Here are the organisations and events that bring Singaporean Lindy Hoppers together.

Frankie Manning visited Singapore in 1998 to organise a Lindy Hop workshop.

NTU Lindy Hop

NTU Lindy Hop is the only dance CCA in Singapore dedicated to Lindy Hop. Based in Nanyang Technological University, NTU Lindy Hop is often many young Singaporeansâ€™ first experience with Lindy Hop. Many members also attend workshops in external Singapore Lindy Hop organisations and even go on to become Lindy Hop instructors themselves.

In 2021, NTU Lindy Hop performed in NTU's virtual Joint Dance Concert. NTU Lindy Hop performance's can be found here.

External Lindy Hop Organisations

Organisations such as B Swing Lindy and Lindy Hop SG organise workshops and performances to bring Lindy Hop to the Singapore public. They have workshops suited for different experience levels, starting from beginner. They even offer classes in other swing and jazz dances such as tap dancing and Balboa.

Social Dancing

Before COVID-19, SwingStation would organise weekly social dancing sessions where Lindy Hoppers could dance together. Social dancing involves Lindy Hoppers dancing improvised partner-dance steps or solo jazz moves, often while a live band plays jazz and swing music.
